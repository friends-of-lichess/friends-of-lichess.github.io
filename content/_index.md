Friends of Lichess is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the Lichess ecosystem. We believe that everyone should have access to high-quality chess tools and resources, forever, without barriers.

## Our Mission

* **Education:** Supporting the use of chess as an educational tool.
* **Libre & Open Source:** Promoting the values of free and open-source software in the chess world.
* **Competition:** Facilitating competitive chess opportunities.
* **Awareness:** Increasing public awareness of the game of chess.
